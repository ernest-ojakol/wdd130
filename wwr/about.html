History

Welcome to WDD 130 Rafting Adventures, where the thrill of whitewater rafting meets the tranquility of nature! Founded in 2005 by outdoor enthusiasts Ernest and Rita Ojakol, WDD 130 began as a small family-owned business nestled in the heart of the picturesque Rocky Mountains. Inspired by their love for adventure and the beauty of the great outdoors, Ernest and Rita set out to share their passion for rafting with the world. Over the years, WDD 130 has grown into a premier destination for adrenaline junkies and nature lovers alike, offering guided rafting trips along some of the most breathtaking rivers in the region. With a commitment to safety, sustainability, and unforgettable experiences, RiverRush continues to create memories that last a lifetime. Whether you're a seasoned rafter seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush or a first-time adventurer looking to connect with nature, come join us at WDD 130 Rafting Adventures for an unforgettable journey down the rapids!
